For our project in DIS we have made a rocket launch simulator. On the following pages you will select a space company, a rocket and a location for your launch.

Rules

1. If you select a rocket produced by the chosen company, the succes rate for your launch will increase with 10%

2. If you select a location where a launch of your chosen rocket has already occured, the succes rate for your launch will increase with 10%
